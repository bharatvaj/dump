### Introduction

Ridesharing or real time ride-sharing is a concept that is on hot wheels in the present day scenario, it is a twinning variation of the usual cab booking service except that it has a sprinkle of the social networking concept over it, instead of full time professional drivers, the app relies on its users who own vehicles to aid the the other users who may not have a vehicle in possession , it rides on the back of the users’ trust , reliability , and feedbacks towards the other users who offer rides , the service provider gets rewarded in some form in return.

On the outline , the app mainly needs the following components to function

A smartphone at both the customer and the service provider’s end

A real-time GPS navigation system that can help with the tracking process

A Feedback forum and a nearby location tracker that can fetch rides for a customer from any desired driver around the tracked location .

The ride-sharing concept was first brought to the market on a full scale by the company called Sidecar, since then it has tasted popularity among drive alone commuters in some parts of the world.

It uses a general optimization algorithm to match rides for the users.

### Vision of the App

RideShare has a greater vision pinned to its core: To promote sustainable environment. Less vehicles means less carbon footprint and less pollution. This will be the stepping stone for a greener earth.

Ride sharing promotes social transportation. It helps people find new and reliable friends and thus makes office travel, an enjoyable and comfortable one.

Ride sharing helps to reduce expenses, reduce the amount of fossil fuel used, reduce the traffic and many more.

Ride sharing helps to find a safe ride and friendly commute partners based on social networks which mainly reduces traffic congestion

### Final delivery Promise

The app when finished will be able to perform the following

Enable users to choose between either ‘offer a ride’ or ‘request a ride’

Remember the users registered from a particular device , and retrieve their details from the database , when logged in from an unfamiliar device

Perform verification/ authentication of a user through techniques of OTP messages , mail verification etc.

Register a fresh user and store their details in the database or retrieve the details of an existing user after a ‘login’ session.

Enable users to send feed of their real time location

Offer users a social network alike account walls where they can change and upload DPs and register feedbacks about their various rideshare experiences.
